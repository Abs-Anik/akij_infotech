The AKIJ GROUP Story

The AKIJ GROUP isn't just a company, it's a Bangladeshi legacy.

In the early 1940s, driven by Sheikh Akijuddin's vision of empowering his nation, a simple trading business took root. This wasn't just about profit; it was about creating jobs and fostering prosperity.

Fast forward, and AKIJ GROUP stands tall as a diversified giant. Textiles, tobacco, food & beverage, cement, ceramics – you name it, they've mastered it. This success wasn't a fluke. It's built on a bedrock of four core values: honesty, punctuality, loyalty, and ethics. These weren't just platitudes – they were lived and breathed, guiding the group's expansion into numerous successful units with over 300,000 employees. 2020 saw another turning point with the formation of Akij Resources Limited (ARL). Led by Sheikh Jasim Uddin, ARL acts as a dynamic cluster serving diverse sectors. Building materials? Check. Shipping? Check. IT solutions? They've got that too. But it's not just about products; it's about serving the nation. From rice and flour to steel bars and IT consultancy, ARL brings necessities and innovation to Bangladesh.

Their grip on the building materials sector remains firm, while their investments in steel showcase their commitment to constant growth. And as the tech landscape evolves, ARL is there, too. Blue Pill Limited and Akij Infotech Limited stand as trusted IT partners, guiding businesses towards a tech-powered future.

The AKIJ GROUP story is more than just financial success. It's about family, values, and building a brighter Bangladesh, one industry at a time.
